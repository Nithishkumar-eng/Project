Training cell

Our college boasts of a strong training and placement cell knitted together effectively by a placement officer, a placement coordinator, a group of experienced faculties of various departments, the corporate world and the Alumni. The mission of providing placement opportunities to the students of all departments without posing a burden of working towards it is achieved through effective and excellent training facilities to students from the first year itself.

A structured framework which serves as the backbone of the training process ensures that students are placed in their fields of interest after a meticulous training process incorporated within the curriculum . The cell helps to provide a continuous and gradual training process which transforms a student admitted with average technical and soft skills into a competent professional ready to face the challenges posed by the industrial recruitment process and succeed confidently.

Once the students needs are identified and categorised, the placement cell provides training on respective areas, catering to the interest of each individual student through aptitude training by IITians, soft skill training by personalities from the industries, honing of technical skills by experts from the college & companies , alumni interactions and webinars that help provide the platform to become recruitment ready. Mock interviews, Group discussions, mock online tests are organised as and when required to enable students to understand their potential , learn and practice so as to tap the opportunities provided.

Our strong bonding with the Alumni, the staunch support of their expertise and experience and the glories of the past have made quite a large number of organisations knock at our doors to recruit our students. We are continuously and cautiously upgrading the training modes and structure so as to be in par with the industrial standards, their expectations which ensures our students get placed in their interested arenas, work with dedication and job satisfaction paving way for a bright career for themselves, thereby achieving the vision and mission of the college.
